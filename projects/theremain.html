Overview

Therem{ai}n is an artificial intelligence enhanced musical instrument (or toy) that responds to, accompanies and inspires you. Participants can play the musical instrument as a normal theremin, and after the play is finished, the therem{ai}n will generate a new melody based on player's input and palys back in the tone and style of theremin.

Source code for this website is available on github.
