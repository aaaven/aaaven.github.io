Moments

Fall 2015


Language: Processing

Code

Overview

Moments is a data visualization project utilizing the location data in pictures taken by phones. As a huge fan of tracking myself, I only get to know the "move" app until recently. The idea of using picture meta data is not only to generate data but also taking advantage of the nature process of taking picture; since people mostly take pictures only for interesting or special moments.
