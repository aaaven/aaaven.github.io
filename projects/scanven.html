Scanven

Spring 2016


Language: Kinect

Overview

Scanven is a 3D scanning project. Since it's scan me- Aven, I coined the word Scanven. The idea is to obtain a digital representation of myself and present it in multiple (interactive) ways.

Documentation

Different techniques are tested for this project: Windows builder with Kinect V2, skannect with Kinect V2 and Skannect with structure sensor. The latter case seems to be best for human body scanning.
